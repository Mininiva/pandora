◈ Tier Progression Atlas ◈

Each Pandora world climbs from raw chemistry toward organised complexity. Tiers mark irreversible phase-transitions — once achieved, the world never falls below.

Primordial Era · T0 → T9
T0Void — sterile chemistry, no meaningful structure
T1Spark — first persistent particle bonds observed
T2Drift — stable dimers; rudimentary chemistry
T3Chain — extended polymers; early replication candidate
T4Network — cross-linked mesh; topology emerges
T5Loop — autocatalytic cycles; self-sustaining reaction
T6Cluster — dense stable aggregates; proto-organelle
T7Cascade — multi-step reaction chains; signal propagation
T8Boundary — first amphiphilic membranes observed
T9Gradient — chemical potential across a membrane
Emergence Era · T10 → T20
T10Compartment — closed lipid vesicle; proto-cell achieved
T11Division — membrane growth and pinch-off events
T12Inheritance — chemical state transmitted across generations
T13Cooperation — multi-cell clusters act in concert
T14Differentiation — distinct roles arise within a colony
T15Signalling — chemical messages between organisms
T16Memory — adaptive response to repeated stimuli
T17Symbiosis — stable inter-species dependency forged
T18Complexity — layered metabolic and structural hierarchy
T19Awareness — environmental sensing and directed motion
T20✦ Sentience — coordinated, purposeful collective behaviour

GRADUATION fires at T13 · worlds above T5 are shielded from destructive perturbations
